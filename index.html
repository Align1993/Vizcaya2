Modern Made Simple

 

We are a Swedish global design studio based in Stockholm, applying a design that knows no limits and promotes a positive change.

Vizcaya: Designer + Developer

We’re built around the concept of a Design & Developer duo — Focusing solely on senior creatives, cutting the overhead and assuring our clients 100% senior competence in every project. Our creatives has more than 7 years of experience designing, developing & launching digital productions.

We apply a holistic approach

Uncovering new behaviors based on user experiences analysis, we tailor products according to our partners needs with applying a multidisciplinary approach. We practice a design that has no limits: using any available tools, sciences, media and techniques for an industrial approach as well as a traditional artisanal production. Our field of action covers product design, brand identity, packaging, UX and UI, scenography, digital as well as interior design.

Committed to design

Our clients come in all shapes and sizes. They vary from smaller businesses launching a new website or product, to huge fashion brands needing a new E-commerce; or larger production companies & advertising agencies wanting to hire some extra digital muscles.
